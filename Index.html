How HydroPro Works

  • Smart Intervals: We divide your work hours by your water goal to find the perfect drinking pace.
  • Wait Timer: The timer stops at 00:00. It waits for YOU to confirm you drank.
  • Visuals: The blue water fills up as your reminder approaches.
  • Persistence: Reloading the page won't lose your data.
  • Reset: Use "Reset Settings" to change your goal or hours mid-day.
